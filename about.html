About KCB Bank Kenya

KCB Bank Kenya is part of KCB Group, one of the largest banking groups in East Africa. For over a century, KCB has supported individuals, SMEs, corporates and governments through accessible and innovative financial solutions. Our purpose is to drive growth that is shared, sustainable and inclusive.

From traditional branch banking to mobile and online platforms, KCB focuses on making banking simple, secure and convenient for our customers wherever they are.
